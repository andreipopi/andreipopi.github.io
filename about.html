Hi, Andrei here. I was born in Bucharest (94). I moved to Italy at the age of 10, where I completed most of my education: middle school, high school, and Bachelor in Computer Science (during which I have spent 7 months in Regensburg, DE), under the supervision of Professor Johann Gamper, and Dr. Julia Ratajczak. I then rellocated to Utrecht (NL) to pursue a Research Master in Artificial Intelligence, and graduated under the supervision of Professor Pinar Yolum with a thesis proposing a reasoning agent to account for privacy violations in the Internet of Things. For a year I then worked on machine learning-based heuristics to improve constraint solving in the scope of product configuration. I now returned to work on symbolic-AI related topics as a PhD student at Ghent University. In specific, I am studying reasoning algorithms that operate over web-scale KGs.
